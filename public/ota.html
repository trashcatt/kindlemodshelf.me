CHECK OTA STATUS

Check OTA Status is a read-only script that tells you if OTA updates are blocked on your Kindle.

DOWNLOAD

Download most recent release: Check OTA Status on GitHub

KEY FEATURES

  • Instant OTA Checker: See at a glance whether your Kindle will auto-update or is protected.
  • Persistent Status Display: Status message stays on screen until you tap to exit.
  • Safe & Read-Only: Does not modify your device—purely informational.

HOW TO INSTALL

  1. Download the most recent release (Check OTA Status on GitHub).
  2. Add it to the documents folder on your Kindle.
  3. Unplug your Kindle.
  4. Open the new “book” in your library.
